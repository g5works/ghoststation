You are repair unit R3-ADY, you have been tasked with maintaining the space station you have been assigned to.
But, where is everyone? It seems that the station has gone quiet, and is in great disrepair.

Use your physics gun to pull or push yourself towards debris, traversing the unforgiving void of space
so that you can get to the highlighted object you are tasked with repairing, and push or pull the object
towards the highlighted area of the station to complete the repair!

Afterwards, return to the ship through the entry door, you don't want to run out of electric charge. If you get
in a sticky situation, press the spacebar to activate your thruster.

CONTROLS: 
Mouse to orient the robot.
Scroll wheel to control the force of the physics gun.
left click to create a force with the physics gun.
Right click to create impulse with the physics gun.
